My Writing Process

01

Intake

Review the client's CV, evidence, and case goals. Map existing materials to each regulatory criterion to identify strengths and gaps.

02

Criteria Analysis

Select the 3–4 strongest criteria. Research applicable USCIS guidance, AAO decisions, and circuit-level precedent for each claim.

03

Evidence Mapping

Draft an evidence summary memo anchoring each exhibit to a specific legal argument. Identify what additional evidence is needed before writing.

04

Drafting

Write the petition with attorney-ready structure — legal standard statement, factual argument, evidence citations, and criterion conclusion for each section.

05

Review

Final QA pass for citation accuracy, argument coherence, regulatory compliance, and elimination of generic or unsubstantiated claims.
